Privacy Policy

Machine Work Log

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Machine Work Log ("the App"), developed by 213 AI Labs, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used.

Information Stored by the App

The App allows you to manage your business records, including but not limited to:

  • Work entries
  • Customer names
  • Customer phone numbers
  • Machine numbers
  • Payment records
  • Other business-related information entered by you

All of this information belongs to you and is stored locally on your device using a local database.

Automatic Backup

The App includes an optional Automatic Backup feature.

To use this feature, you must first choose a backup destination folder using Android's built-in file or folder picker and grant the App permission to write backup files to that location.

Once enabled, the App automatically creates backup files according to your backup settings. Backup files are saved only to the folder that you selected. This location may be:

  • Your device storage, or
  • Your personal cloud storage (such as Google Drive) if you choose such a location using Android's Storage Access Framework.

The App does not upload your information to any server operated by the developer. The developer does not have access to your backup files or the data they contain.

You may disable Automatic Backup or change the backup location at any time from within the App.

Biometric Authentication

The App includes an optional App Lock feature.

If enabled, the App uses your device's built-in biometric authentication (fingerprint, face authentication where supported) or your device screen lock (PIN, password, or pattern) to protect access to the App.

The App never accesses, stores, or transmits your biometric information. Authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. The App receives only a success or failure result.

Data Collection

The App does not collect your personal or business information.

The App does not operate any servers and does not automatically upload, synchronize, or transmit your information over the internet.

Third-Party Sharing

The App does not sell, share, or transmit your data to the developer or any third party.

If you choose to save backup files to a third-party storage provider such as Google Drive, the backup files are stored directly in your own account. Your use of that storage provider is governed by its own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

The App does not access, analyze, or share your backup files with anyone.

Data Deletion

You remain in full control of your data.

You may permanently delete your information by:

  • Deleting records within the App.
  • Clearing the App's storage through your device Settings.
  • Uninstalling the App.
  • Deleting any backup files stored in your selected backup folder or cloud storage.

Uninstalling the App does not automatically remove backup files that you have previously created.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for business use by adults and is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Security

Your information is stored on your own device and in backup locations selected by you. You are responsible for protecting your device, backup files, and any cloud storage account that you choose to use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page along with a revised "Last Updated" date.
