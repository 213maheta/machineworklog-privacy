Privacy Policy

Machine Work Log
Last updated: July 5, 2026

Machine Work Log ("the App"), developed by 213 AI Labs, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how that information is used.

Data Collection and Storage

The App allows you to record work entries, customer information (such as names and phone numbers), machine numbers, and payment records for your own business management purposes.

All data is stored locally on your device using a local database. The App does not operate any servers, does not transmit your data over the internet, and does not upload or back up your information to any cloud service.

Your data is never shared with the developer, third parties, analytics providers, or advertising services.

Biometric Authentication

If you enable the optional App Lock feature, the App uses your device's built-in biometric authentication (such as fingerprint) or screen lock (PIN, password, or pattern) to protect access to the App.

Authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. The App does not access, collect, store, or transmit your fingerprint or any other biometric information. It only receives a success or failure result from the operating system.

Third-Party Sharing

Since the App does not transmit any data, no information is shared with any third party.

Data Deletion

Because all information is stored locally on your device, you can permanently delete your data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the App, or
  • Clearing the App's storage through your device's Settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for business use by adults and is not directed toward children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page along with an updated "Last updated" date.
